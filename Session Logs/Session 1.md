The group is headed towards a Castle from Fort Smith, Arkansas.
* They are scouting out the route to ensure it can be declared safe for crossing.
* It's a ten day journey, and the scene opens on the evening of day 9.
* So far, everything has been calm and it's been an easy journey
That night, Drippy takes first watch, Ras takes second, and FLUX takes last watch.
* Up in the middle of her watch, Ras leaves the group.
* FLUX is awakened by his Docent, and he wakes Drippy up -- he calls out to Ras immediately.
* She comes back, and points out a strange glowing structure nearby.
* They sneak up on the structure, quite unsuccessfully, but Ras is able to use her abilities to detect a ladder inside, possibly heading down belowground.
* Ras tries to open the lock... also unsuccessfully.
* She observes that it seems to suck in magical energies
* Drippy is not deterred and is able to melt the lock with ease.
* There is indeed a hatch with a ladder that leads downward.
	* The material is stamped with Chai-Town Steel, definitely coalition.
* They fail disastrously to sneak in, and a fight breaks out.
	* This appears to be some kind of [[Coalition substation]].
	* Ras severs the leg of one, and Drippy gets shot.
	* It turns out Ban is in the back—FLUX and Ban combo to break a chain and partially free him, just moments after Drippy sets the hallway **[[on fire]]**.
	* Ras slices and dices for two **wounds** on the **Officer** and takes out another Coalition soldier.
The session wraps up with a wounded Officer, a stunned soldier, and a remaining soldier in the control room.
